About College

National Institute of Technology Sikkim (NIT Sikkim or NIT SKM) is a public engineering and research institution near the city of Ravangla in Sikkim, India. It is one of the 31 National Institutes of Technology in India and has been declared as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. It is an autonomous institute and functioning under the aegis of Ministry of Education, Government of India.

Academic Excllence

NIT Sikkim excels academically with top-tier faculty, innovative research, and advanced facilities across its engineering and science programs.

Infrastructure and Campus Facilities

Academic Facilities: The campus houses well-equipped laboratories, advanced computing facilities, and smart classrooms The institute offers separate hostels for male and female students,

Placement and Industry Connection

NIT Sikkim has a strong placement record, with students landing roles in top companies. The institute actively partners with industries to offer internships, live projects, and industry visits.
