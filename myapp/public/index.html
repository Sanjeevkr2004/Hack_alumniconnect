Why Alumni Connection

Alumni connections open doors to networking, mentorship, and opportunities,
fostering personal growth and professional advancement for all.

Mentorship and Guidance

Alumni who have experienced similar academic and career paths can offer advice, mentorship, and support to current students and fellow graduates.

Personal and Professional Development

Engaging with alumni can lead to skill-building workshops, seminars, and events that enhance both personal growth and professional skills.

Shared Experiences and Reunions

Alumni connections allow for reminiscing about shared experiences, organizing reunions, and fostering a sense of belonging to a larger community.

Giving Back to the Institution

Alumni can contribute to their alma mater through volunteering, fundraising, or offering internships, creating a lasting impact on future generations.

Networking Opportunities

Alumni networks provide a valuable platform for building professional relationships, exploring job opportunities, and collaborating on projects.

Stay Informed and Inspired

Alumni networks help keep members informed about university news, events, and developments, while also inspiring them with success stories and achievements of fellow graduates.

Udgam

Udgam is the cultural fest of NIT Sikkim, celebrating creativity through dance, music, drama, and art. It brings together students and artists nationwide for lively competitions, performances, and workshops, showcasing culture and youth.

Convocation

Convocation is a formal graduation ceremony where degrees and honors are awarded. It celebrates students' academic achievements and marks their transition from student life to their future endeavors.

Abhiyantran

Abhiyantran is the technical fest of NIT Sikkim, featuring competitions, workshops, hackathons, and seminars. It brings together tech enthusiasts to showcase skills in coding, robotics, and innovation, fostering creativity and collaboration.

Induction

Induction is an introductory program for new students to familiarize them with the campus, its culture, and academic life. It includes sessions on university policies, resources, student activities, and mentorship opportunities.

SIH (Smart India Hackathon)

Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide competition encouraging students to develop innovative tech solutions for real-world challenges.
